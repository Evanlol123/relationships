Heatlhy or Unhealthy Relationships

This website is an imformative website educating other teens on the topic of healthy and unhealthy relationships as well as how to access help when needed

Healthy Relationships Characteristics

  1. Respect

    2. Being cautious about someone's boundaries.

  2. Trust

    3. Trusting someone that they won't purposefully hurt you

  3. Honesty

    4. Believing someone won't lie to you.

  4. Support

    5. Assisting eachother during hard times.

  5. Fairness

    6. Just being nice to each other

  6. Communication

    7. Being comfortable to talk to each other.

Health Benifits of Healthy Relationships

  1. Lower rates of anxiety
  2. Lower rates of depression
  3. Higher self-esteem
  4. Greater empathy
  5. More trusting and cooperative relationships

Unhealthy Relationships Characteristics

  1. Disrespect

    Crossing Someone's Boundaries

  2. Manipulative

    3. Controlling Someone

  3. Dishonesty

    Telling lies

  4. Yelling

    Being loud toward someone

  5. Jealousy

    Envious of someone's achievements

  6. Harming

    Harming someone for no reason

Emotional Risks of Being in an Unhealthy Relationship:

  1. Anxiety/Depression
  2. Trama
  3. Isolation

Maintaining Relationship Skills

  1. Communicate clearly to avoid confusion
  2. Be kind and supportive
  3. Be honest, but not brutally honest
  4. Respect each other to help build feelings of trust
  5. Gain trust for transparent communication and a deeper connection

Actions to protect from unhealthy/abusive relationship:

  1. Help

    If you are in an unhealthy relationship you should get help from others

  2. Warning signs

    Learn the warning signs of an abusive relationship

  3. Avoid the abuser
